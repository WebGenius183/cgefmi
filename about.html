About Us

Christ Gospel Evangelical Fire Ministry International, led by a dynamic and anointed man, Pastor Rilwan Olowo Adamu and his amiable wife, Pastor Mrs. Precious Abiodun Rilwan. Surrounded by a Board of Trustees and dedicated, respected leader.


Whilst this church is still attaining heights, they have succeeded in spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ far and near, Under three years (2020 till date) of establishment, with the backing of the Holy Ghost, the Church has been able to plant four daughter churches which are situated at various places such as Ikoya, Akure, Abakaliki and Adamo to the glory of God which further proves that their evangelical nature backed up by Christ Himself is at work.
